Example
input
2
4
1 2 3 4
2
1 4 0
1 3 -100
5
2 1 2 5 4
3
2 4 3
2 5 -2
1 3 1

output
-99 -98 -97 4 
2 1 2 5 4 

Note
In the first test case: b0=[1,2,3,4]; b1=[1,2,3,4]; b2=[−99,−98,−97,4].
Thus, the lexicographically minimum array is b2.
In the second test case, the lexicographically minimum array is b0.
